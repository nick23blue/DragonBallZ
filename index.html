About the Show

Dragon Ball Z is a japanese aInime show. It follows the story of a warrior named Goku, who protects the Earth against evil. I've been a fan of this show for as long as I can remember. When I watch each character train to increase their strength, it helps motivate me to workout. Plus, the video game series has always been one of my favorites.

This show has always been a fan-favorite for many years. IGN has rated the show 78th out of 100 animated shows. It has also ranked 50th on Wizard's Magazine's 100 greatest animated shows.
